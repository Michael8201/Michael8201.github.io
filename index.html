Cyclistic Bike-Share Analysis (24 Months)

This dashboard explores two years of Cyclistic bike-share usage to uncover patterns in rider behavior, seasonality, membership trends, and ride duration. The goal is to help stakeholders understand how casual riders differ from annual members and identify opportunities to increase membership conversions.

Key insights include peak riding seasons, differences in weekday vs. weekend usage, and how ride length varies across customer segments. These findings support data-driven recommendations for marketing, pricing, and customer engagement strategies.
